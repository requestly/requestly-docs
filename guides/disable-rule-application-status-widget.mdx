---
title: Disable Rule Application Status Widget
label: Disable Rule Application Status Widget
slug: disable-rule-application-status-widget
description: >
  Hide the on-screen widget that appears when a rule is applied by turning it
  off in the settings.
visibility: PUBLIC
---
<img src="/images/disable-rule-application-status-widget/53b78339-0cff-4ac6-96bb-9ac743d217c7.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    **Navigate to the Requestly Settings page,** Open the Requestly extension and click on the **Settings** tab in the left sidebar.

    <img src="/images/disable-rule-application-status-widget/fc595c39-6ddd-403c-9d46-9880db361e2b.png" align="left" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate Widget Option">
    In the Global Settings menu, locate the **Show widget when rule is applied** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Disable the Widget">
    Click the toggle to disable the widget. This will stop the visual indicator from showing when rules are applied.

    <img src="/images/disable-rule-application-status-widget/afeed1ff-bb27-4d94-878c-0561d2827181.gif" align="left" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
