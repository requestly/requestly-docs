<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Requestly Dashboard Page">
    Navigate to the [Requestly Dashboard](https://app.requestly.io/) in your browser.

    <img src="/images/can-i-log-in-to-requestly-using-my-browserstack-account/6f90557b-fa95-45df-8708-c76155567d9f.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the “Sign in” Button">
    Click the **Sign in** button on the top right corner of the page.

    <img src="/images/can-i-log-in-to-requestly-using-my-browserstack-account/80aeb1ec-3e6f-436a-a6b3-bea05a06b443.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Email Address">
    Enter the **email address associated with your BrowserStack account**

    <img src="/images/can-i-log-in-to-requestly-using-my-browserstack-account/f42a5f28-ff40-4366-8070-5f862a5ab6bf.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authenticate via BrowserStack">
    Depending on your status with BrowserStack, the next screen will vary:

    * **If you already have a BrowserStack account**, you will be prompted to log in with it.\
      If you're already logged into BrowserStack in your browser, you’ll be logged in to Requestly automatically

    <img src="/images/can-i-log-in-to-requestly-using-my-browserstack-account/d3962719-262c-4987-a1b4-0bb22ac241d7.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />

    * If you don’t have a BrowserStack account, you’ll be guided to create one.

    <img src="/images/can-i-log-in-to-requestly-using-my-browserstack-account/4bfcbdee-d0a3-4ce4-b5a2-0d796042ff79.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
