Experiencing trust issues with certificates when using Requestly for intercepting Safari traffic or setting it as a system-wide proxy? This might be due to the invalidation of Requestly's Root Certificate.

**Error Indication:** An error message will appear in your browser when it's traffic is intercepted by Requestly:

<img src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-prod-files-secures3us-west-2amazonawscom-8e04dd23-74cd-4c44-8158-4238eac359d1-0a66faab-9671-4187-b55d-192c04226ed9-screenshot_2023-11-30_at_75745_pmpng/public" alt="" align="left" fullwidth="false" />

 

**Solution Steps:**

1. **Access Settings in Requestly:**

   1. * Open the Requestly desktop application.

      - Click on the Settings Icon located at the top right.

      <img src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-prod-files-secures3us-west-2amazonawscom-8e04dd23-74cd-4c44-8158-4238eac359d1-e641b751-07b7-4bc6-a2b4-3ca84dabf685-screenshot_2023-11-30_at_72433_pmpng/public" alt="" align="left" fullwidth="false" />

       

2) **Regenerate SSL Certificates:**

   1. * Inside the Settings, find and click the `Regenerate` button.

      - This action regenerates the SSL certificates.

      <img src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-prod-files-secures3us-west-2amazonawscom-8e04dd23-74cd-4c44-8158-4238eac359d1-bd07855a-16c7-4761-be3d-6664b9f9899e-screenshot_2023-11-30_at_74433_pmpng/public" alt="" align="left" fullwidth="false" />

3. **Restart and Verify:**

   1. * The application will automatically close to complete the regeneration process.

      - Relaunch the app and start intercepting traffic again. The previously encountered error should now be resolved.
