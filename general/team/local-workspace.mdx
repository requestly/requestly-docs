Local workspace in Requestly, like <a target="_blank" href="/general/others/workspace/how-to-get-started-with-shared-workspace">
Team Workspace</a>

, is a type of workspace. The key difference is that it stores all your data on your local system, ensuring full privacy for your data. This means your collections, requests, and environment data are stored only on your device and cannot be accessed by anyone else.

  Local Workspace is currently in **early beta** and available only for the API Client **on the desktop app**. Since it‚Äôs still in development, some features may not work as expected.

<Steps>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Select ‚ÄúJoin or Create Workspace‚Äù">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Local Workspace and Configure">
    Select **‚ÄúLocal Workspace‚Äù** as the workspace type. Enter a **workspace name** and choose a **folder** where all configurations and API data will be stored.

## **How to Delete a Local Workspace**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace Menu">
    Click the **current workspace name** in the top left to open the workspace dropdown.

    ![Workspace Menu Pn](/images/workspace-menu.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Workspace Settings">
    Select the Local Workspace you want to remove and open its **Settings**.

    <Tip>
      You can also delete any related files or folders stored on your device if you want to clean up everything linked to that workspace
    </Tip>
    ![Workspace Settings Delete Pn](/images/workspace-settings-delete.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete Workspace">
    Click **Delete Workspace** and confirm the action by entering the workspace name. Then click the **Delete Workspace** button to complete the removal.

    ![Delete Workspace Modal Pn](/images/delete-workspace-modal.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
