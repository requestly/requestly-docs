***

Pinning rules in Requestly allows you to quickly access and manage your rules directly from the Chrome toolbar, streamlining your workflow without the need to open the Requestly app. This feature is particularly useful for toggling rules on or off with ease.

## Benefits of Pinning Rules

1. **Quick Access:** Access frequently used rules directly from the toolbar.

2. **Simplified Management**: Toggle rules on or off with a single click.

3. **Improved Workflow**: Avoid the need to open the Requestly app, streamlining your debugging or testing tasks.

## How to Pin a Rule

To pin a rule in Requestly, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Rule">
    Navigate to the list of rules in your Requestly dashboard.

    <img src="/images/pinning-rules/3e2d329c-0d77-492f-8f06-c64af97efae1.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the ‘Pin’ Option">
    On the left side of the rule, you will see a ‘Pin’ icon. Click on it to pin the rule to your toolbar. You can also pin a group.

    <img src="/images/pinning-rules/4dccbb80-f9ea-44f1-9e4e-7417b0199428.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once pinned, the rule will be accessible from the Requestly icon in your Chrome toolbar.

### Accessing Pinned Rules

To access and manage your pinned rules:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Toolbar">
    Click on the Requestly icon located on the Chrome toolbar.

    <img src="/images/pinning-rules/78f80b03-ecbe-4afc-a174-353ab1b7d53c.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Toggle Rules">
    You will see a list of your pinned rules. From here, you can toggle each rule on or off with just one click.

    <img src="/images/pinning-rules/d1f4b56c-d888-4dc1-a736-7378aa063d86.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
