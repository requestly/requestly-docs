Simplify your workflows by sharing and managing rules effortlessly within your team or across your devices. Requestly provides tools to make it seamless. Explore different ways to share your rules:

  <Card title="Share in Workspace" href="/general/http-rules/sharing/share-in-workspace">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Shared List" href="/general/http-rules/sharing/shared-list">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Download Rules" href="/general/http-rules/sharing/download-rules">
