Bruno is a powerful API development tool for designing, testing, and managing APIs. Requestly can seamlessly import collections and environment files exported from Bruno, making the transition hassle-free.

### Exporting Collections from Bruno

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Bruno">
    Launch the Bruno application on your device.

    <img src="/images/bruno/dcb31800-10cf-4e57-9d0b-b46717ac4f1d.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Collection">
    To export a collection, click the ellipsis (…) next to it and select **Export** from the dropdown menu.

    <img src="/images/bruno/7fa11526-75f2-4bbc-b456-2c3127ea2443.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Export Format">
    In the export dialog, select Bruno Collection

    <Tip>
      In Bruno, when you export a collection as a file, the environments are exported within the same file. There is no need to export environments explicitly.
    </Tip>

    <img src="/images/bruno/0538e6fd-21c4-449a-9fa7-7df600494558.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the File">
    Click **Export** and save the file to your preferred location
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Importing into Requestly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open API Client">
    In Requestly app, navigate to the **API Client** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Import">
    Click the **Import** button in the sidebar header and choose **Bruno** from the import options.

    <img src="/images/bruno/8ca1e7bf-e6fb-4fb3-893b-2de3a60a7573.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Files">
    In the upload modal, select and upload each exported Bruno collection or environment file individually.

    <img src="/images/bruno/0cda84cc-c7b4-44bb-b3ae-2284e1442661.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Finalize Import">
    Once the files are processed, click **Import** to finalize each migration.

    <img src="/images/bruno/6c8cafa5-f0fd-4962-8a5b-4b4a774cab6c.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
