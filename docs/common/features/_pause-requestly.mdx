If you want requestly rules **do not** get applied on your websites or webapps,
then you can pause the requestly extension.

#### Follow the steps:

1. Click on requestly icon in toolbar (right-side of the addressbar).
2. Hover over the dropdown show in popup on top saying **Requestly running**.
3. Click **Pause requestly**.
   <br />
   <img
     width="70%"
     alt="Pause requestly from popup"
     src="/img/docs/popup-pause.webp"
   />

#### Also there is another way to directly pause requestly from toolbar.

1. Right click on requestly icon in toolbar (right-side of the addressbar).
2. Click **Deactivate Requestly**.
   <br />
   <img
     width="40%"
     alt="Deactivate requestly from toolbar"
     src="/img/docs/deactivate-requestly.webp"
   />

<br />

:::note

You need to resume it to make requestly work again.

:::
