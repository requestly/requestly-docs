You can check the version of Requestly you are using in three ways:

### **Using the Requestly Extension Popup**

1. Click on the **Requestly** extension icon in your browser’s extension list.

2. A popup window will appear.

3. Look at the **bottom-left corner** of the popup to find your current Requestly version.

### **Checking on the Requestly Dashboard**

1. Open the **Requestly Dashboard** by visiting [app.requestly.io](https://app.requestly.io).

2. Look at the **bottom-left corner** of the dashboard.

3. Your current Requestly version will be displayed there.

<img src="/images/how-to-check-your-requestly-version-in-the-browser/7fcd0daa-2483-4e22-bb2f-4371d635ff18.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />

### **Checking via Browser Extension Settings**

You can also find the Requestly version through your browser’s extension settings:

* **Google Chrome:** Go to `chrome://extensions/?id=mdnleldcmiljblolnjhpnblkcekpdkpa`

<img src="/images/how-to-check-your-requestly-version-in-the-browser/73359b8f-8899-41e0-8920-08909cf89da8.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />

* **Mozilla Firefox:** Open `about:addons`, find Requestly, and check the version.

<img src="/images/how-to-check-your-requestly-version-in-the-browser/5d2119d8-73b3-4ae2-8643-53fe57a0f874.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />

* **Microsoft Edge:** Go to `edge://extensions/?id=ehghoapnlpepjmfbgaomdiilchcjemak`

Following these methods, you can quickly find the Requestly version you are using.
