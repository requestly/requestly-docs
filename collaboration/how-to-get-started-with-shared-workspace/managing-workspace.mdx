### Renaming a project

Projects can be renamed by following these steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click on your project name">
    In the Requestly dashboard, locate and click on your project name at the top.

    <img src="/images/managing-workspace/accab303-1b56-4aad-ab0a-5d980d0e3b8a.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to project settings">
    From the dropdown or navigation menu, select **Project Settings**.

    <img src="/images/managing-workspace/70a6a9b8-aab7-47a0-ac53-0e70b834b877.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit the project name">
    In the **Project Settings** section, find the **Project Name** field and enter the new name

    <img src="/images/managing-workspace/92a2fbd9-e625-454f-a48e-30924ebb0d55.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    <img src="/images/managing-workspace/45d67893-3956-419a-bab8-f157379c8bd6.png" align="center" fullwidth="false" />

    Click the **Save Changes** button to apply the new project name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Delete a project

Projects can be deleted using the **Delete Project** button in the project settings
